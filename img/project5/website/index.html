Ældre Sagen

what

This website will walk you through of our concept of bringing the Elderly and Children closer together

how

By showcasing our process and by creating and planning the best festival in Odense, enjoyable by all ages! :)

why

Nowdays kids between the age of 6-16 spend too much time on computers (PCs, Smartphones, Consoles, etc.) neglecting everything and everyone around them in the process.

who

We are Multimedia Design students at EAL, Odense and this is our first project in 2nd semester.

The Project

[1]

Focus

Our festival will focus on the elderly and children, the main goal here is for them to spend quality time TOGETHER. The festival will be held and setup by the volunteers and members of the organization, each of them contributing their own ideas and their own games/hobbies.

[2]

Set-up

We want to have several workshops, sitting activites and "active" activites set up all across the choosen terrority of where it will be held. Everyone will be welcomed of course, but the main attraction is for our choosen target group. A sort of scavanger hunt within each activity set you for the main prize(s) in which only them can compete.

[3]

The Hunt

In order to win in this scavanger hunt, you have to complete each personaly choosen activity (considering the conditions of the elders) in order with your helper/grandkids. At the entrace, if you signed up before hand, you will get your first clue of where to go. You will get clues throughout the whole thing after each completed task. The clues will be easy to solve so the kids could enjoy them too.

[4]

Technologies
