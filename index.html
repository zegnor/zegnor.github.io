BØRNEBASKET ODENSE

My very first project revoled around BØRNEBASKET ODENSE. They were my and my team's very first client, so it was both exciting and scary at the same time! The requirements were easy, increase the membership count. We quickly come up with a campaign plan considering their budget, values, vision and mission. The main focus was to get both the parent and children attention using Online and Offline advertisments. With the mascot, we wanted to attract more girls, while with the logo, boys. The colour scheme was based off of big leagues where these specific colours were more common. Ended up with a flyer, instead of a re-design of their webpage as non of us knew how to code at the time.

I also created an animated web banner, gave me the vibes of 2005. Was my first attempt using Animate.

  • Date: September 2016
  • Service: Creating a Campaign

Augmented Reality for Økolariet

We were asked by Økolariet to create a multimedia solution including either VR or AR for their museum. The goal was to make insects seem more desirable as a food source and to educate the children about these possibilities along the way. Me and my current team went with AR, as it was more suitable for their requirements. It's easy to creat, set up, relocate and is cost effective.

Our gameplay is straight forward and easy, we went with this idea, because the target group were children between 8-14. There will be a stack of cards next to a TV screen, where kids will be able to pick any insects printed on them, when said card is facing the camera, an animated 3D illustration of a dish, made out of that specific insect will be shown. On the back of each cards, there will be accurate fun facts and information about each bugs.
