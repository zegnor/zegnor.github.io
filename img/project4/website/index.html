About

Each one of us are coming from a different musical background we decided to use this particular strength to team up and create something new and unique. A music, deeply rooted in rock but with melodic and atmospheric textures all brought together thanks to rock instruments like poetic 70's guitar arpeggios, powerful piano and warm drums. The music is based on the legacy of iconic rock band with the necessary touch of modernity.

Our process is pretty simple. Practice. Practice. Practice. We want to chase our dreams, not to catch them. To make and build a community around our music

This band is a laboratory of experimentation between us. There is no rules for composing and playing except one: HARMONY. Harmony is the leitmotiv of Summit. It is present every time. Between us playing together, in the songs we write and with the public when playing on stage.

Our Project

Setup

We are a Classic rock setup. 5 guys, 5 instruments: Vocals, keys, guitar, bass, drums. The lyrics are deeply inspired by philosophy. The different background musicality from each of us gives us an interesting sonority and makes them unique.

Harmony

We each achieve 7 titles written by three of us. The choice of written in group complement their expectation about create something new, personal group identity and unique. All of songs get a conduct line which is harmony between note, song and lyrics.

Bonding

More than sharing music, we want to share a really unique and special moment with the public. We want people to feel what we feel when we play.

We definite the band as a project, an adjective which is match perfectly with the music goal we have expected, but so far it became much more than that. We became a small family.
